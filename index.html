Sr. R&D Electrical Engineer at Sandia National Labs from August 2023 to present

  • I lead the design and integration of the Gas Transfer System, including the electrical interface for vacuum pumps, manometers, gas analyzers, and valving systems, as well as writing firmware and GUI control interfaces. Electrical Design Engineer at KLA from Jun 2022 to August 2023

  • In this role, I designed functional test fixtures for stage motion controller PCBs and developed embedded firmware for them. Additionally, I designed a high-speed stage motion controller board and collaborated with the external PCB layout team to ensure optimal power and signal integrity using HyperLynx simulation software.

Sr. Reliability Hardware Engineer at Analog Deices from July 2019 to June 2022

  • I collaborated with cross-functional teams of design and product engineers on various products, including BMS, voltage regulators, ADCs, and DACs, to ensure all products exceed reliability and performance standards. I designed reliability circuits for Burn-in and HAST applications and developed ESD and latch-up testing methodologies and characterization plans in accordance with JEDEC and AECQ standards.

Engineering: R&D Product Development at Nordson March from October 2017 to July 2019

  • I was responsible for integrating EFEM (Equipment Front End Module) systems with plasma modules and collaborating with customers for various plasma cleaning applications. I also tested various vacuum chambers and RF power delivery matching networks for optimal plasma treatment, uniformity, and throughput.
