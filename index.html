Tin Thurein

Tin Thurein is an enthusiastic Electrical Engineering Student, he holds B.S degree from University of California, Santa Cruz.

He is a strong collaborator and communicator with cross-functional teams, and a conscientious multitasker able to handle simultaneous electrical design and prototyping tasks with full accuracy and efficiency.

Tin has interest in integrated circuit design and simulation, digital signal processing as well as IoT Products.

As an undergraduate student, Tin has taken various opportunities to take as much engineering classes as he could fit into his schedule to obtain as much engineering experience as possible. Tin has experience working on class projects such as prototyping AM/FM radio, designing adjustable voltage/current power supply unit, audio amp, and microcontrollers based projects, which has helped him gain deeper insights on RF designs, PCB designs, simulation, prototyping, and coding.

Tin also has experience designing small projects involving Arduino, and IoT devices such as Particle Electron as well as 32-bit ARM processor based Cypress 5LP board. Having worked with FPGA boards as a class project, Tin has gained a better understanding of how they worked and being able to program.

Tin has deepened his interpersonal experience by participating in local Burmese (Myanmar) community fundraising events, especially helping in stage settings, lighting, and sound systems. These volunteering works have helped him improved in communication skills and teamwork abilities. Tin enjoys working with a team as he finds it gratifying to be able to solve challenging problems with peers.
